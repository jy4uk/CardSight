## Introduction

### What is CardSight?

**CardSight** is an all-in-one platform designed for trading card vendors and collectors to manage their inventory, track sales, and handle trades efficiently.

#### Key Features:

- **Inventory Management** — Track your entire card collection with detailed information including card name, set, condition, pricing, and images
- **Sales Tracking** — Record sales with multiple payment methods and view revenue analytics
- **Trade Management** — Process customer trade-ins and trade-outs with automatic inventory updates
- **Insights Dashboard** — View metrics like total inventory value, profit margins, and sales trends
- **Barcode Support** — Scan barcodes to quickly add items to cart or look up inventory
- **Multi-Game Support** — Supports Pokémon, Magic: The Gathering, Yu-Gi-Oh!, and One Piece TCG

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Navigate to **cardsight.vercel.app**
2. Click the **"Get Started"** button on the landing page
3. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Email** — Your email address (used for login and password recovery)
   - **Username** — A unique identifier (3-50 characters, letters, numbers, underscores, or hyphens)
   - **First Name / Last Name** — Your display name
   - **Password** — At least 8 characters
   - **Beta Code** — Enter your beta access code (required during beta period)
4. Check the box to accept the **Terms of Service**
5. Click **"Create Account"**

### Logging In

1. Click the **"Log In"** button on the landing page
2. Enter your **email** and **password**
3. Click **"Sign In"**

> **Tip:** Your session will remain active. If you're logged out, simply log in again to access your inventory.

### Forgot Your Password?

1. On the login screen, click **"Forgot password?"**
2. Enter the email associated with your account
3. Check your inbox for a password reset link
4. Follow the link to create a new password

## Adding Cards to Your Inventory

### Method 1: Quick Add (Single Card)

1. From the main inventory view, click the **"+ Add"** button in the top navigation
2. Fill in the card details:
   - **Barcode** (optional) — Scan or type the barcode for quick lookup
   - **Card Name** — The name of the card
   - **Set Name** — The set/expansion the card belongs to
   - **Card Number** (optional) — The card's collector number
3. Set the **Game** (Pokémon, Magic, Yu-Gi-Oh!, One Piece)
4. Select the **Card Type**:
   - **Raw** — Ungraded card
   - **PSA/BGS/CGC Slab** — Graded card (enter certification number for auto-lookup)
5. Set the **Condition** (for raw cards) or **Grade** (for slabs)
6. Enter pricing:
   - **Purchase Price** — What you paid for the card
   - **Sticker Price** — Your selling price
7. Click **"Add to Inventory"**

### Method 2: Intake Page (Bulk Purchases)

1. Navigate to the **Intake** tab using the bottom navigation (mobile) or top menu
2. Click **"Record Purchase"** to open the purchase modal
3. Add multiple cards by scanning barcodes or entering details
4. Cards are staged before being added — review your list
5. Click **"Add All to Inventory"** to complete the batch

### Using PSA Auto-Lookup

For graded cards, CardSight can automatically fetch card details:

1. Select **PSA Slab** as the card type
2. Enter the **PSA Certification Number**
3. CardSight will automatically populate:
   - Card name and set
   - Grade
   - Market pricing data

## Selling Cards

### Selling a Single Card

1. Locate the card in your inventory grid
2. Click on the card to view details
3. Click the **"Sell"** button
4. Enter the **Sale Price** (defaults to your sticker price)
5. Select a **Payment Method**:
   - Credit Card
   - Cash
   - Venmo
   - Zelle
   - Cash App
6. Click **"Complete Sale"**
7. The card is automatically removed from your inventory and the sale is recorded

### Bulk Selling with Cart

1. Enable **Multi-Select Mode** using the checkbox icon in the header
2. Tap cards to select them (they'll show a checkmark)
3. Selected cards appear in your **Cart**
4. Open the Cart drawer to review selected items
5. Process the sale for all items at once

### Using Barcode Scanning

CardSight supports hardware barcode scanners:

1. Connect a USB barcode scanner to your device
2. Scan a card's barcode from anywhere in the app
3. The card is automatically added to your cart
4. Continue scanning to add multiple items quickly

## FAQs & Troubleshooting

### Q1: Why can't I log in?

**Common causes and solutions:**

- **Incorrect password** — Use the "Forgot password?" link to reset
- **Session expired** — Clear your browser cookies and try again
- **Network issues** — Check your internet connection and refresh the page

### Q2: My barcode scanner isn't working

**Troubleshooting steps:**

1. Ensure the scanner is in **HID (keyboard emulation) mode**
2. Click somewhere on the page to ensure the app has focus
3. Try scanning a barcode — it should appear as typed text
4. If using Bluetooth, ensure the scanner is paired and connected

### Q3: A card I added isn't showing in my inventory

**What to check:**

- **Filters active** — Clear all filters using the filter button
- **Search active** — Clear the search bar
- **Recently added** — Sort by "Newest" to see recent additions
- **Page refresh** — Pull down to refresh or click the refresh icon
